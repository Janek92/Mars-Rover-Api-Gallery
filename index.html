introduction

In order to search for signs of past life and explore Mars in 2003 were sent to rovers.
One of them was Spirit which mission was planned at 90 days. Finally Spirit outlasted on Mars over six years and found evidences that this planet was somewhen much more earth-like and helped scientists better understand Martian wind. In May 2009 Spirit stuck in soft soil at a site called "Troy" and after many months of testing NASA ended efforts to free the rover end on May 25, 2011 mission was closed.

Opportunity is a Spirit twin rover sent in July 7, 2003. The main goal of its mission was search signs of ancient water and as just like Spirit much more exceeded his 90-days mission plan to almost 15 years. The most important discoveries was that long ago at least one area of Mars stayed wet for an extended period and that conditions could have been suitable for sustaining microbial life. In result of Mars-wide dust storm its location was blanketed in June 2018.

Curiosity landed in Mars' Gale Crater of August 5, 2012. Since that day Curiosity's mission is still ongoing and its goal is to determine whether the Red Planet ever was habitable to microbial life.
